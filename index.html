About WISE Food Ethiopia

Connecting nutrition science, community education, and innovation to improve health outcomes across Ethiopia.

Our Mission

WISE Food Ethiopia is dedicated to helping people stay healthy by connecting them with evidence-based food and nutrition education, as well as practical culinary skills training. We work to create an inspiring and collaborative environment that connects government organizations, key stakeholders, and volunteers‚Äîencouraging them to work together, share knowledge, and exchange skills and expertise to improve nutrition outcomes.

Our Vision

We envision stronger food and nutrition policies, strategies, and implementation across Ethiopia, achieving measurable progress by 2030. Our goals include:

  • Increasing awareness of nutrition's impact on health
  • Promoting access to healthy and sustainable foods
  • Empowering youth and women through education and practical skills
  • Building resilient urban and rural food systems

NutriMap AI: Personalized Nutrition & Recipe Intelligence

Putting evidence-based, affordable nutrition guidance in the hands of every urban resident.

üçé Nutrition¬∑ü§ñ AI Intelligence¬∑üèòÔ∏è Community

‚ùå The Challenge

  • Lack of personalized nutrition guidance for urban Addis residents
  • Food choices often based on price, not nutrition
  • Limited awareness of Food-Based Dietary Guidelines (FBDG)
  • No easy way to align meals with individual nutritional needs
  • High malnutrition despite adequate food access

‚úÖ Our Solution

  • AI analyzes individual nutritional needs based on local Ethiopian foods
  • Personalized recipe recommendations using FBDG
  • Integration with local market prices via aggregated data
  • Telegram bot for urban residents and health workers
  • Nutrition tracking with adaptive guidance

How It Works

üìä Personalization Engine

Users input basic health information (age, weight, activity level, dietary preferences). NutriMap AI builds a personalized nutrition profile aligned with FBDG recommendations.

üç≥ Smart Recipe Generator

AI generates daily meal recommendations using local Ethiopian ingredients‚Äîinjera, teff, legumes, vegetables‚Äîmatching both nutritional goals and budget constraints.

üí∞ Market Price Integration

Market data shows ingredient costs at local Addis vendors, helping users make affordable nutritious choices.

üì± Telegram & SMS Access

Low-bandwidth access via Telegram bot ensures accessibility for populations with limited internet connectivity.

Features & Benefits

  • Nutritional Balance: Ensures meals meet daily requirements for calories, protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Cultural Relevance: All recipes use traditional Ethiopian ingredients and cooking methods.
  • Budget-Friendly: Meal plans adapt to user budget constraints.
  • Health Tracking: Monitor nutrition goals over time with adaptive recommendations.
  • Multi-Language: Available in Amharic, Oromifa, and English.
  • Expert Backing: Recommendations based on WHO guidelines and national FBDG.
  • Integration with WISE Programs: Connects to culinary training and community education.
